Our Story

“There is nothing better than drinking in good company, but wouldn’t be great if there was a naturally lighter alcoholic beverage?

We feel passionately about changing the way we enjoy alcohol. So after some masterful experimentation, we finally created the perfect combination. A lightly sparkling and naturally bittersweet spritzer crafted in an English Winery.

Invigoratingly light and lower alcohol, Emba is our refreshing answer to those muddled memories and expanding waistlines after ‘one too many’. At 4% ABV and less than 90 calories a serve, one bottle of Emba is equivalent to one unit of alcohol. Simple.

Taking great pride in our taste buds, we created a beverage uncompromising on quality and character that intertwines botanical and bitters flavours with exciting English wine.

We are suitable for vegans, preferentially source local ingredients and avoid all things artificial allowing you to feel morally superior to boot.

Social drinking just got better.”

Matt Clark, Founder
